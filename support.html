Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my PDF files saved?

After creating a PDF, you can save it to any location using the "Save to Files" button. You can also find your PDFs in the Files app under Scanify PDF, or share them directly from within the app.

What image formats are supported?

The app supports all image formats that iOS can handle, including JPEG, PNG, HEIF, and more.

How can I reduce the PDF file size?

In the PDF settings, lower the "Quality" and "Image Scale" sliders. This will reduce the resolution and compression quality, resulting in smaller files.

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. Scanify PDF works entirely offline. No internet connection is needed to use any feature.

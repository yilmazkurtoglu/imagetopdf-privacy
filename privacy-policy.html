Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Image to PDF does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

1. Overview

Image to PDF is a utility app that converts images to PDF documents. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Photos or images you process
  • PDF documents you create
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage statistics or behavior tracking

3. Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, which are used exclusively for the features described:

  • Camera: Used solely to capture photos that you choose to include in your PDF documents. Photos are not transmitted or stored outside the app.
  • Photo Library: Used solely to select existing images from your device for PDF creation. We do not modify or upload your photo library contents.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • PDF files are saved to the app's Documents directory on your device.
  • User preferences (such as filter settings and language selection) are stored locally using UserDefaults.

No data is sent to external servers or cloud services.

5. Third-Party Services

Image to PDF does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, the app is safe for use by all age groups.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
